Join Us

You can help us and many in Nepal just from your couch or sofa. You can be a designer, marketer, coder, writer, problem-solver, or anyone else and are interested in helping people make better use of technology, be digitally and data literate, then join us via:

Slack (Say hello and introduce yourself) Github (You can start looking at projects and contribute by resolving issues or suggesting enhancements) Facebook Twitter
We also encourage you to explore our website, learn about our projects, and read the “Code of Conduct” policy. If you are an Amazon shopper, you can help us by shopping through smile.amazon.com and selecting to support Code for Nepal.
